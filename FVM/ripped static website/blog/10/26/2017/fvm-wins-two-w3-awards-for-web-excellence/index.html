Wow! FVM is beyond proud to have been recognized with two Silver 2017 W3 Awards for our work on behalf of digital pathology startup Proscia and campus safety non-profit Clery Center.

The W3s — which are judged by the Academy of Interactive and Visual Arts (AIVA) and honor creative excellence on the web — awarded Proscia.com for copywriting and CleryCenter.org for non-profit website.

So a big pat on the back to FVM’s storytellers, designers, and developers who work passionately and skillfully to connect B2B brands to their audiences. And a HUGE thank you to our clients whose incredible businesses and trusting partnerships challenge and inspire us every day to come up with truly creative solutions.

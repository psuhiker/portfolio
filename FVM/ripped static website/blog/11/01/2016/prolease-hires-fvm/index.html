We’re thrilled to announce that ProLease Software has become our new client.

The fast-growing Real Estate and Facility Management software company has signed on for brand positioning, visual identity, web design, and full 2017 integrated marketing strategy, as it looks to strengthen its offering and grow market share. And with over 600 clients, a much-loved product, and an excellent reputation, we can’t wait to support ProLease in taking on the competition.

ProLease is one of many great businesses we’ve been fortunate enough to add to our roster recently. Others include:

  • Lightpath, a major New York regional business telecom company offering high-performance connectivity for medium to enterprise-level businesses, who hired FVM for corporate and product brand positioning, visual identity, web design, and campaigns.
  • The Clery Center for Security on Campus, a national non-profit organization that works with college and university communities to create safer campuses, who joined us for brand positioning, visual identity, web design, and marketing strategy.
  • Library Systems and Services (LS&S), a national company that partners with local government to improve operations and services for their Library systems, who came onboard for brand positioning, web design, and marketing strategy.
  • National Catholic Educational Association (NCEA), the world’s largest private-professional education organization, who took us on for brand positioning, and visual identity.

We love working with such a diverse range of B2B businesses. Finding unique ways to solve varied marketing obstacles across complex industries is what gets us up in the morning.
